Biography

I am a first-year PhD student at the School of Informatics, Xiamen University, advised by Prof. Zhihong Zhang and Dr. Qinggang Zhang (Hong Kong Polytechnic University). In 2023, I received a B.Mgt. from South-Central Minzu University. In 2024, I founded the GenIRAG Team and have close work with Chentao Zhang, Chenfeng Zheng, and Yuxuan Hu.

My research interests include Information Retrieval (IR), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Knowledge Graph (KG). Currently, my research topics is Trustworthy Retrieval-Augmented AI and Its Application.
